About Qamryn

Hi y’all! My name is Qamryn, and I am a senior studying Information Science with a certificate in Leadership. Outside of academics, I am a member of the Wisconsin Cheerleading team, which takes up much of my time during the school year. I am also a member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., and I currently serve as the President of the National Pan-Hellenic Council on campus.

I chose to take this class because I am aware of the ongoing lack of representation—and frequent misrepresentation—of diverse human groups within computer science and technology fields. I wanted to better understand why these issues are becoming increasingly important today, how we can work to address them beyond simply increasing diversity numbers, and how to critically confront my own assumptions about technology and innovation.

My favorite “tech” topic right now is learning about Big Data and artificial intelligence, particularly their ethical implications, environmental impact, integration into daily life and non-technology industries, and the ways they are reshaping human thinking and behavior.
