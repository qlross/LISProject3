Kimberly Bryant

Kimberly Bryant is an electrical engineer who founded Black Girls Code, which focuses on exposing young, underrepresented girls to coding and computers. The bulk of her career has been spent as a Biotechnology Engineer at companies such as Genentech and Pfizer. She is a champion of breaking barriers, fighting for equity, and prioritizing societal advancement by giving back to her community.
